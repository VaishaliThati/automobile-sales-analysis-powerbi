## **📊 Automobile Sales Analysis Dashboard**

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing automobile sales data using Power BI to identify revenue trends, sales patterns, and key business drivers.

The primary objective of this project is to perform in-depth **sales and revenue analysis**, which constitutes the core (≈90%) of the project, along with supporting insights on product performance and customer behavior.

An interactive multi-page dashboard was developed to transform raw data into actionable business insights for better decision-making.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

• Power BI (Dashboard Development & Visualization)
• Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
• DAX (Measures & Calculations)

---

## 📊 Key Analyses Performed

• Data exploration and cleaning using Power Query
• Sales and revenue trend analysis (time-based analysis)
• Product performance analysis (revenue contribution & pricing insights)
• Customer behavior analysis (repeat customers, segmentation, revenue contribution)
• KPI development (Total Sales, AOV, Total Orders, Customer Metrics)

---

## 📈 Dashboard Overview

The Power BI dashboard consists of three pages:

• **Sales Overview** – Focused on revenue trends, KPIs, regional performance, and deal size contribution
• **Product Performance** – Analysis of product lines, top-performing products, and pricing insights
• **Customer Insights** – Customer behavior, retention patterns, and revenue contribution

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

• Revenue is highly concentrated among a few product lines and high-value deals
• Sales trends show variation over time, indicating possible seasonal patterns
• A small group of customers contributes a significant share of total revenue
• Repeat customers play a crucial role in overall business performance
• High-value products generate strong revenue even with lower quantities
